█ Welcome to SecBSD project!

We've developed a security-focused UNIX-like operating system based on the renowned OpenBSD.

SecBSD is focused on the needs of hackers, pentesters, hacktivists, cybersecurity professionals, and privacy-conscious individuals.

At the core of our development process is the same philosophy as OpenBSD, emphasizing robust security, privacy protection, and strong cryptography by default.

Join a community of like-minded individuals and gain valuable insights from SecBSD and OpenBSD users through our irc server or official forum for discussing SecBSD and related topics.
